- Use the ESC key to pause the game or go back.
- Press the play button in the menu to start the game.
- You can also set the difficulty in the main menu.
- When you start a game, you need to deploy your ships.
- Press the play button after you finish deploying.
- The goal of the game is to hit all the ships of the oppenent before the oppenent hit all yours.
- Score will be taken after each game.
